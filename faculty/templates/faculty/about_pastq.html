About pastq

Revolutionizing the Way Students Prepare for Exams

Our Mission

At pastq, we believe that every student deserves the opportunity to excel in their studies. Our mission is to revolutionize exam preparation by providing accessible, efficient, and effective study tools that cater to learners of all levels.

We strive to create a platform that not only helps students ace their exams but also fosters a deeper understanding of their subjects, promoting long-term learning and academic success.

The pastq Journey

2023

Conception of the idea for pastq during a late-night study session.

Early 2024

Development of the pastq platform begins, as I venture into making the Idea come to life.

Mid 2024

Beta launch of pastq, with overwhelmingly positive feedback from early users.

Late 2024

Official launch of pastq, revolutionizing exam preparation for students worldwide.

Meet the Creator

Alameen Dalha

Founder of pastq

As a student who struggled with exam preparation, Alameen envisioned a platform that would make studying more efficient and effective. With a background in computer science and a passion for education, he founded pastq to help students worldwide achieve their academic goals.
